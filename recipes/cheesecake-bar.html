Cheesecake Bar

Description

A sweet treat with a toasty graham bottom and a rich creamy top

Ingredients

Cheesecake Filling

  • 16 ounces cream cheese
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tblsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tblsp vanilla extract
  • 1/8 tsp salt

Graham Cracker Crust

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs very fine
  • 9 tblsp melted butter
  • 1/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/8 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F
  2. Spray and line a 9 x 13 baking pan with parchment paper
  3. In a bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, melted unsalted butter, brown sugar, and salt. Press the crust into the bottom of a 9x13 pan and pack it in tightly. Set aside.
  4. Using a stand mixer with a paddle attachment or handheld beater, add cream cheese and sugar to the bowl and beat on medium speed until it is creamy and smooth. Scrape down the sides of the bowl.
  5. Then add the eggs, cornstarch, sour cream, vanilla extract, and salt and mix until creamy and smooth again. Scrape down the sides of the bowl as necessary to make sure everything is incorporated and there are no clumps.
  6. Pour the cheesecake batter over the crust and spread evenly.
  7. Bake for about 40 to 45 minutes in the center rack of your oven.
