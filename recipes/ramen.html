Ramen

Description

A Japanese noodle soup consisting of a soup base, pork, eggs and vegetables

Ingredients

  • 4 cups pork broth
  • 6 oz ramen noodles
  • 2 eggs
  • green onions
  • 1 lb chashu pork belly
  • 1 cup miso tare

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients
  2. Boil the ramen noodles
  3. Fry chashu pork until lightly browned
  4. Place tare in bowl
  5. Ladle in pork broth and stir to mix
  6. Add the noodles and pour in more broth
  7. Top with egg, pork and green onions
