Potato Gratin

Description

A French bake of layered potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 stick butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tblsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 lbs potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded Gruyere cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Grease baking dish.
  2. Combine cream, butter and garlic in saucepan. Bring to simmer then remove from heat and mix in herbs and spices.
  3. Arrange half of the potato slices in the baking dish. Sprinkle cheese and pour cream mixture on top. Add second layer of potato and cheese.
  4. Bake in oven for 30m or until golden brown and bubbly.
